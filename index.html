The Perfect Eye
Experimental Video, Animation, 1920x1080
This work is an exploration of my imagination of what happens when true self acceptance and understanding occurs. I am trying to capture the overwhelming release one feels after letting go of things like self-doubt, negativity, etc. I chose to display this through the eyes because there is an idea in various cultures and theories that one's eye is a direct link to their soul. So this animation is a visual representation of what one's eye would look like as a result of lifting one's limiting aspects of their persoanlity/nature. « Previous Work Next Work »
